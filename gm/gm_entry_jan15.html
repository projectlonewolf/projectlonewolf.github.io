Plan, Ideas, Research & Simulations

Description

For my social project goal I chose, I will be starting a project to teach and show people how genetic engineering can be used for amazing things, thus supporting my stance on genetic engineering.

Ideas

The actual project could be several different things, but in talking to friends and family, I came up with the idea of a website where people could run simulations of different forms of reproduction, and see how people would change throughout generations based on a predetermined set of parents and rules.

Aditionally, I think there should be a way where the user could also edit some of the people on the simulation's genes and see how that plays out. My brother is also really interested in genetics, so I think it would be amazing to be able to work with him on this.

Another idea I had was to create posts on social media where I could share fun facts about genetic engineering and how it can be used to make a difference in the world. Especially with the use of GMO's; also I read about this somewhere, but I remember there is some sort of bacteria that creates insulin, which would be super interesting to write about. So maybe I could create like an Instagram and Tiktok account and just post some fun facts that would make people interested, and also share my stance, but also just educate people about what genetic engineering is and how it can be used for good.

Also, I think I could create the social media accounts, and then relate them to the website simulations, as I think that would take a bit longer, and since they're both similar, it would work well to have a preexisting audience for both.
