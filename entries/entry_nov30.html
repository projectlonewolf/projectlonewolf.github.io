Description

Today we finished the first episode a docu-series named "Unnatural Selection" about the implications and impact of genetic engineering.

Reflection

Q1: What are some of the positive benefits of using genetic engineering?

I see almost infinite possible benefits, it could cure diseases, make people healtheir, un-age people, and possibly even cure death. It could even allow for people to live in other planets!!!

Q2: What are some concerns or possible negative outcomes of using genetic engineering?

I think what could be the most negative outcome is people being forced to be a certain way, or not have a choice to what happens to their body. Additionally, another possible concern is that people who don't have access to this technology will be "left behind" in this "genetic arms-race"
