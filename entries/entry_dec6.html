Chimera Activity

Description

Today Isabella (my groupmate) and I worked on the Chimera Activity, where we picked genes from one animal and used them to create a new animal.

We chose the cat as the base of the new animal, and picked new genes from cows and rats to create a new, *better* cat, who has all the benefits of the original cat, but also has the perks of the other animals.
