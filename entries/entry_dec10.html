Description

Today we watched and took notes on the Human Nature documentary!

Notes

In today's class, we worked on the avatar activity, where we "created" our "perfect" human, who would fit in best with society.

I worked with Theo, and at the start we disagreed with each other, and I thought that we should've made the avatar be the "middle" of all social groups and standards and society, but Theo insisted that we should have just made the avatar as something society would view as "perfect", which is what we ended up doing.

We shared what we did with the class, and I was a little surprised, but not really with how basically everyone created a straight, white, male. This although I expected it, I was still a bit surprised.

Additionally, the use of power and privilege in society is something that we should all really think of, and be aware of and be able to understand and appreciate our own power and privilege over other people who are not.

In one of our discussions, we talked about the difference of privilege between men and women as when women behave a certain way, they are slut-shamed by society, but when men behave in the same manner, they are "praised" for their actions.
